City Stories

Tweet

Created by Wylie L. Goodman

I'm a Writer in New York, NY who currently works at Partnerships for Parks. Previously, I've worked at McKinsey & Company, HalfthePlanet.com, Oxygen.com. I earned a B.F.A. from Tisch School of the Arts, New York University.

Description

An interactive app that allows NYC residents to record stories of no longer than 3 minutes about significant life events that happened to them in the city and geolocate those on an interactive map. Users can search the map based on key words, listen to stories, and view brief profiles of contributors or become contributors themselves.

I'm making this app in order to create an emotional, narrative map of New York City. You can think of this app like a Time Capsule, but one with feelings, that offers users a way to experience the city through the real-world stories that take place here each day. City Stories is different from historical walk apps in which a disconnected, present-day narrator describes past events. Here, the city comes alive through the voices of the actual people who lived the experiences. Likewise, these stories celebrate city life through its emotional resonance, rather than solely through fact-based events.

I'm looking for help with development and design. I know there are audio recording apps that let people use their mobile phone to record and upload narratives to the Web (e.g., Voice Record, Audioboo, Broadcaster). I also know that there are geolocation apps that let people tag themselves to a specific location (e.g., FourSquare). I don't know how to combine these functionalities into a single proprietary app that also allows people to create profiles and offers searchability. I want to work with a developer/designer team so that funding for this project can be pursued through Kickstarter.

Goals

  • Design a mobile app called City Stories.
  • Develop a mobile app called City Stories.
  • Fundraise for app development and release on Kickstarter.
