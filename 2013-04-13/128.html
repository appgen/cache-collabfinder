LocalSqr - Recyclebank for Local Food

Tweet

Created by Alastair Greer

I'm a Designer in New York, NY who currently works at WellBe Solutions. I earned a B.A. from Farnborough College.

Description

LocalSqr (http://www.localsqr.com) is mobile technology for local food to help Farmers Markets, local farmers, producers and artisans grow their business.

Through our "Go Local Corporate Initiative" (http://wellbesolutions.com/solutions/implement-track/), we work with companies to improve the health of their employees by educating them on healthy nutrition, getting them back in the kitchen cooking from scratch, and rewarding them for buying local food.
If there is a corporate cafeteria, we also help them source local where possible whilst adding healthier recipes to the menu.

Food and health are inextricably linked, and LocalSqr is built on a very simple hypothesis -
"Cooking from scratch more often using healthy, clean fresh ingredients will improve your health."

We engage companies large and small in the support of stronger local food systems and healthier lifestyles. This presents them with Triple Bottom Line benefits (people, planet, profit).

We have some traction, (http://www.psfk.com/2013/04/saks-fifth-ave-employee-health.html)

Looking for Collaborators
- Building rewards platform. LocalSqr points for purchasing local, participating in "Go Local" and composting.
- Artists/Designers to bring a new design direction to App and media
- Writers fine tuning the message
- Connect with Local food vendors and introduce them to LocalSqr.
- Share and support great local, healthy food!

Goals

  • Redesign of public facing and "Go Local" media.
  • A workable rewards platform.
  • Find key members of the team / testing with GrowNYC/NYC.gov / pull corp client specific data from rewards platform.
