Versus Live - The first location-based RPG - Play In Your Real World!

Tweet

Created by Matt A-Row

I'm a Designer in Los Angeles, CA who is currently freelancing. Previously, I've worked at Total Immersion, Axium International, Inc., Creative Planet, Warner Bros. Entertaindom.com. I earned a B.S. from University of the Pacific.

Description

Versus Live is a mobile, social RPG game. The only way to advance in the game is to get out and meet people, face-to-face, in the Real World. It's like the assassin game: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assassin_(game) but without the complex structure and administration. This game is SIMPLE. No complex setup. Pick a side. Pick a User name. Upload a profile pic. Done. Game on!

People are social animals. We need real contact with real people in the real world. This is not about traditional gamers locked to their screens for hours. This is not about exchanging photos and keeping up with friends's status updates. This is about making new friends (and enemies ;-), where you live and work and play. It's a fun and unique way to "break the ice" and meet new people. The ONLY way to advance in the game is to get out and ENGAGE with other players in-person, in the Real World. Sound cool? I think so, too.

I have specs for iOS/Android as well as for the server-side, which I think Python will cover. I need those specs vetted and ultimately, built.
I also need an artist to create logos and website graphics. I have some ideas and would love to collaborate to make something awesome.
I'm putting a team together to launch a Kickstarter campaign. The idea is that if the Kickstarter campaign succeeds, everyone gets paid.

Goals

  • Create the website to support the Kickstarter Campaign
  • Launch the Kickstarter Campaign re-using graphics and other resources from the website
  • Launch Versus Live before 2013 is over!
